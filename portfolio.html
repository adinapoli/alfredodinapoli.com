My Portfolio

Laetus Framework

Laetus is a parallel C++ framework that aims to alleviate the burder of writing plain OpenCL, providing an higher level of abstraction and several improvements over the standard computational model of OpenCL. Laetus is my senior thesis project.


Expi.it

Expi Espresso Italia is a courier that delivers parcels within the city of Rome and all accross the world. This is the site I've realized for them.


Clj3D Library

Clj3D is a Clojure graphic library for manipulating 3D and 2D objects. It aims to be the Clojure standard in 3D and 2D rendering.


Newsface

Newsface performs a non-invasive search inside your Facebook profile and finds your closest friends, extracts a bunch of keywords based on your friends tastes and finally searches the web, bringing you news.

League of Legends Custom Skin

League of Legends is a MMO game from the creators of DOTA. I've realized a custom skin for a playable champion, using Photoshop and Maya for a partial 3D model rework.
